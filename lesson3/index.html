Site Purpose

Todays Weather provides local & long-range weather forecasts, weather reports, maps & tropical weather conditions for locations worldwide. It pretends to give not only weather information, but also adequate activities, suggested places to spent time, recommended locals to eat and buy clothes, etc. All these features linked to the weather.
